I Wish

There's a knock on the door
Has monster come back
Your cresent eyes dark
Prove your nights apart
Make it silent, let nobody know
What do you think they would do
When they see the tears flow
Would they come to you
Or would you grow more afar

The chatter in your skull, winning a bit everytime
You stow away the words, but they come back harsh in a tide
The mirror reflecting you back, as the person you want to hide

Here a storm, there a draught
Your screams drowned down, your breath cut-off
Eyes filling like the deep deep seas, but drying away like water springs
You find your ways through the rounding maze
And come out in a soulless daze

Your heart in a desperate pound, watching as your world burns down
Thoughts like a merry go round, always making so loud a sound
Fury, a feeling so warm and strange
It does all harm and leaves just pain

Once you lose it, it never comes back
So find something that stays intact
Like warmth, and joy, in a world you never tried
A world full of broken hearts, just like you
A world of hearts still beating through

You hope that the sun rises, never goes down, even if just for a day
That would be just enough to run through and find your way
Move the curtains aside, let the rays hit you
And this is all for you because you can shine so bright -By Mish & Ridz
