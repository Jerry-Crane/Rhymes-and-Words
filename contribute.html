Contribute

Rhymes and Words

Do you write too? Then you can contribute to this website by adding your own works!

You can send your write-up to the email ID- riddle.asimo@gmail.com and we shall put it up on our website!


Specifications for the write-up:

  • Choose a unique topic and avoid using common topics.
  • If you use a common topic, make sure to add a fun spin in the writing.
  • The write-up should be 100% original and not copied from anywhere.
  • Have fun and play with your creativity!

Format of e-mail:

  • The document must be in a .doc or .pdf format.
  • The subject of the E-mail should be "Entry for Rhymes and Words"
  • The topic & genre of the write-up and the details of the author should be mentioned in the e-mail.
  • The document should then be attached to the mail.
